About Me

A few of my favorite things are programming, designing software, music and soccer. While I am primarily a Software Engineer, I believe that without design, any software is inclomplete and that design is 50% of the software.

With ample experience as a Software Engineer, I have been programming since I was 13 years old. Starting with HTML and Visual Basic, I went on to learn C++ and MySQL by the time I got to college, and have since worked with several languages and frameworks. C++ still remains one of my favorites.
Two of my favorite fields in Software Engineering are Machine Learning/AI and Full-stack development, while I also enjoy working with Embedded Software, and UX Design.

Engineering doesn't come without design, and luckily, I have had great experience in the same. At my summer internship in 2017 at Little Black Book, Delhi, I worked as an iOS developer and designer. Under the guidance of my mentors, I learned a lot about working in the field and about good engineering and design principles. In summer of 2018, I intered at Pine Labs, where I created a test suite for a bank simulator in C# and C++, and then worked with the iOS team.

I am currently in my third year of Computer Science at the University of Virginia, and intend to graduate in May of 2020. I am passionate about the future technology and making a difference in the world with it.
